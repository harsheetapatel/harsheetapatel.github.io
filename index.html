The TRUE Crime: Behind America’s Deadly Fascination

True crime coverage has skyrocketed in popularity on social media, whether it be amateur investigators trying to find their own clues or others fascinated by heart-wrenching stories. However, the almost cult-like fascination can have detrimental consequences on the family and police organizations trying to solve the case. In this podcast, we explore how America’s interest can turn deadly and how to ethically enjoy true crime content without warping the story or victims.
